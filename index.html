Yipeng Gao

I am currently a third-year master's student at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), where I am advised by Wei-Shi Zheng. Before coming to SYSU, I obtained my B.E. degree at the South China University of Technology. I am also fortunate to have an in-person internship with Prof. Cihang Xie at the University of California, Santa Cruz.

My research interests mainly lie around Computer Vision, with the goal of developing general and generalizable vision systems. Nowadays, I focus on vision-language foundation models and their applications. I believe it is one of the essential tools for facilitating people's interaction with modern vision systems and enhancing the quality of their lives. Now, I am committing to take a small step toward this vision.

I am always open for research discussions and collaborations :). Also, I am looking for a potential Ph.D. position enrolling in Fall 2024. Welcome to reach out to me if interested.
